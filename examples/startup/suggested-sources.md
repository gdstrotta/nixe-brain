# Suggested Sources — Startup Knowledge Base

## Tier 1 — Ingest Immediately

**Active client documentation**
Meeting notes, scoping documents, proposals, delivery summaries for each
active client. One ingestion per client onboarding conversation.
*These are your highest-value sources — they capture real-world learning.*

**Strategic frameworks in use**
The strategic frameworks your team actively uses (Blue Ocean, Lean Startup, etc.).
Ingest your own application of these frameworks, not just their generic definitions.

**Principles and patterns you've discovered**
Any documented "rules we've learned" from client work, post-mortems, or retrospectives.
These become your most durable Concept pages.

---

## Tier 2 — Ingest Early

**Pipeline and prospect context**
Meeting notes and background research for active prospects.
Context that helps you personalize proposals.

**Product flows and architectures**
Documentation of the automation flows, integrations, and architectures you've built.
Especially what was reusable across clients.

**Competitive landscape**
Your own analysis of competitors, market positioning, and differentiation.
Not generic market reports — your specific strategic view.

---

## Tier 3 — Ingest Progressively

**Validated books and frameworks**
The books your strategy is based on. Ingest your own notes and highlights, not
the full book. Focus on the frameworks you actually apply.

**Industry vertical intelligence**
Research on specific verticals you serve or want to target. Ingest your own
synthesis, not raw reports.

---

## Suggested First Wave (5 sources)

1. A detailed summary of each active client (context, pain points, deliverables, status)
2. Your company's core principles or operating values
3. A summary of your most replicable product or flow
4. Notes from your most recent strategic planning session
5. A prospect pipeline overview
